FAQ

What is GroupArena?

GroupArena is a collection of party and social games you can run inside any group chat — quick rounds, roleplay, or big social deduction games.

How do I add my own games?

We plan to add a submission feature. For now, contact the developer to suggest games.

Are there age restrictions?

Yes — Freaky Pack content is 18+. Be responsible and verify participants' ages before playing.

Can I use GroupArena on mobile?

Yes. The site is mobile-first and works in mobile browsers.
