How to Play

Follow these steps to play any game on GroupArena:

1. Choose a Game

Open the Games page and browse categories. Click a game to see full details, description, and rules.

2. Copy the Rules

Use the copy button to copy the entire game info — name, players, description and rules — then paste into your group chat.

3. Setup & Roles

Some games require a moderator or roles (e.g., Werewolf/Mafia). The rules will note if a moderator is needed.

4. Age-restricted content

Freaky pack games are for 18+ only — respect age gates and group rules.

Enjoy your games in your whatsapp group

Tips

  • Start with Quick Games to get everyone warmed up.
  • Use the Shuffle button to pick a random game.
  • For immersive games, plan a moderator and time to play.
